vehicle service





what is service A and service B?

The first required service for your vehicle is service A, which is due around 1 year of purchase.


here is what's included with Service A.

  • Oil filter replacement

  • Tire inflation check and correction

  • Brake component inspection

  • Reset maintenance counter


Approximately 2 years since your purchase it will then be time to bring your vehicle for service B.


Here is what's included with service B.

  • Oil filter replacement

  • cabin dust/combinaton filter replacement

  • brake fluid exchange

  • Tire inflation check and corrections
